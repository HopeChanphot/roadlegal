```text

Branch: gh-pages
Folder: / (root)
Save
```

## Reporting Issues

Please include:

- Country or jurisdiction.
- Question or offence.
- Vehicle class.
- Expected result.
- Actual result.
- Source link if available.
- Screenshot for UI issues.
- Whether you used local backend, cloud backend, or GitHub Pages.

## Legal Notice

RoadLegal provides informational traffic-law and road-safety guidance. It is not legal advice. Fine schedules change and can vary by local authority, road class, vehicle class, and case facts. Verify urgent or disputed matters with the relevant traffic authority or legal professional.
